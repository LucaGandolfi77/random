## Chapter 16 – The Last Dawn

Outside, the city that had once thrummed with markets and laughter was now a mosaic of broken tin roofs and smoke‑smeared windows. Amina paused, wiping sweat from her brow with the back of her hand. “I believe in us,” he said, the words thick with conviction. “And I believe that the last dawn will bring us whatever we need.”
