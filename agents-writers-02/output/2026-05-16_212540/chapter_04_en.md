## Chapter 4: The Fire in the Basement

Mara and Lio moved through the streets like ghosts, their boots leaving faint imprints in the dust that had settled on the cracked pavement. The air smelled of ozone and earth, a perfume that tasted of something that had once been alive.

The building’s façade was a mosaic of shattered glass and graffiti, a testament to the chaos that had swept across the city. The two of them pressed deeper into the building. Their footsteps echoed in the hollow space, the sound swallowed by the emptiness that had once been a home, a school, an office.
