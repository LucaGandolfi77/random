Title: **The Echoes of a Sunlit Farewell**  
Premise: In the rain‑kissed streets of late‑century Naples, a young violinist named Elena finds solace in the city’s forgotten churches, where she hears the ghostly strains of a love story long silenced by war. When a wandering poet named Luca stumbles upon her music, they forge a fragile bond that awakens memories of a past love that once promised eternity. As the city rebuilds, Elena must choose whether to lay her heart down for the haunting melody of something lost or to let the music guide her toward an uncertain, hopeful future.
