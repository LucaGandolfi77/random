# Title: The Reflected Self
**Genre:** Slow-burn Romantic Drama  
**Logline:** As a charismatic architect faces the decline of his legendary career, he must choose between the messy reality of aging and connection, or a self-destructive obsession with perfection—until a woman who sees his flaws offers him a different way to exist.
