## 2. Sensory & Image Detailing

**Current:** “Who knew you had a taste for complications?”

*Why?* The italics stress the pun (taste → food → mayo), reinforcing the thematic echo.

**Current:** “What if we actually *fix* something? Instead of just… quoting?”  **Suggested Revision:**  
> “What if we actually built something?” he asked, eyes flicking to the stacks of discarded setlists. “Not just... quoting poetry.”  *Why?* “Built” ties back to his fixer identity and eliminates the ellipsis, which can feel indecisive.
