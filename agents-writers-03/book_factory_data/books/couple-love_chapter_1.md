## 1. Opening Paragraph – Prune Redundancy

> *Original*: “The wind carried whispers through skeletal trees, their branches like grasping fingers against a bruised twilight sky. *Suggested rewrite*:

> The wind whispered through skeletal trees, their limbs stretching like grasping fingers against a bruised twilight. Once the crown jewel of the northern kingdoms, Ashenvale was now a graveyard of splintered trunks and gray soil—poisoned by the Blight of the Shattered Star. *Why*:  - Shorter, punchier sentences keep the rhythm moving.

## 2. Kael’s Inner Conflict – Show, Don’t Tell

> His armor, once polished silver, was now scarred and dulled. The scratches were not merely battle‑worn; they traced jagged veins of grief that seemed to pulse beneath the steel.

*Why*: Connects the visual scar to the emotional scar, making the metaphor visceral.
