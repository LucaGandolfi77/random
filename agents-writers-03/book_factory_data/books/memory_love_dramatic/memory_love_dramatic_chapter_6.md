## 2. Section‑Level Checklist (What to Verify After Editing)

| **Section** | **What to Check** | **Why It Matters** |
|-------------|-------------------|--------------------|
| **Opening & Atmosphere** | Sensory details are vivid but not overloaded. Keep one strong image per beat. | Prevents the reader from feeling “info‑dumped.” |
| **Character Introductions (Miri & Evelyn)** | Are their motivations crystal clear within the first 2–3 lines? Do we feel their power dynamic? | Sets up stakes and why the reader should care. |
