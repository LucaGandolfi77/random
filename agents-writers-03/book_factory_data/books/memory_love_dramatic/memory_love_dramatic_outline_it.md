**Titolo (provvisorio):** *Setola e Circuito*  

**Premessa in una riga** – In una megacity illuminata a neon dove ricordi vengono estratti, comprati e venduti, una esperta contrabbandiera di ricordi ruba una lettera d'amore da lunga tempo dimenticata. La lettera la conduce verso l'altera erede del conglomerato che monopolizza il commercio di ricordi, e loro partenariato riluttante trascina entrambi in un romance pericoloso che potrebbe riscrivere il mastello della città del passato — e loro futuri stessi.

### How the Outline Meets the Template

- **Genre/Sub‑genre & Style** – Slow‑burn romance set against a high‑concept sci‑fi backdrop; each chapter layers observation, subtext, and changed behavior.  
- **Dual Close Third‑Person POV** – Alternating tight focalisation on Miri and Evelyn provides interior transparency while keeping the other's perspective just out of reach.  
- **Central Conflict** – Desire grows while corporate duty, family loyalty, and the illegal‑memory economy block commitment.  
- **Promise of Tension & Payoff** – Every chapter escalates either intimacy or obstacle, includes a misreading/revel beat, and ends with a tangible new risk.  
- **MQ** – Vulnerability (characters exposing hidden scars), self‑concept vs. connection (Miri as "outlaw," Evelyn as heiress), timing/readiness (the letter's age mirrors their readiness), love amid ordinary pressure (train rides, boardrooms, cafés).  
- **MQS** – Attraction is shown through the way they handle the letter, their nervous habits, and the way each changes her own defensive posture when near the other. Obsts arise from concrete forces (raids, legislation, family expectations) rather than "talk it out."  
- **Supporting Cast** – Board members, sibling enforcer, crew of archivists, and the memory‑guard provide pressure without dominating the narrative.
