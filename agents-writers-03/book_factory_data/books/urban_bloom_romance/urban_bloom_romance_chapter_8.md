### Quick Checklist for Future Edits  

1. **Punctuation consistency** – Use em‑dashes or commas deliberately to control rhythm.  
2. **Avoid dangling modifiers** – Make sure the subject of each clause is clear.  
3. **Show, don’t tell** – Layer sensory details (the click of heels, the sagging porch, the buzz of the phone) to thicken atmosphere.  
4. **End on a hook** – A trailing ellipsis or an unfinished thought makes readers want to turn the page.
