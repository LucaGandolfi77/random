# Ink‑Tide Rising

Emotional focus: intensify Elisa's grief‑driven ambition, expose the terror of losing one's own name, forge rivalry tension between Elisa and Maeron, amplify the chorus’s parasitic menace as a living embodiment of forgotten pain.

The living veil swallows the sunrise, and each stroke Elisa makes erases a piece of herself—until the ink begins to seep into her skin, marking her with a living scar.

## Scene 1

Goal: Show the veil’s accelerated spread and the world’s panic.. Conflict: Ink floods the market district, turning stone into mutable canvas; citizens panic as their spoken pleas become echo‑phages.. Turn: Elisa orders the palace guards to seal the district, but the ink seeps through the stone, forcing her to use a desperate stroke to create a barrier.. Image: A river of black ink coursing through cobblestones, swallowing a vendor’s stall while a child’s laughter turns into a snarling, ink‑spattered echo‑phage..

## Scene 2

Goal: Introduce Maeron and his dangerous proposal.. Conflict: Maeron offers Elisa a glass‑fruit that can bind the veil in one strike, but it will erase a memory she has not yet lost.. Turn: Elisa hesitates, then smashes the fruit, feeling a cold shock as a vivid memory of her childhood home flickers out—revealing the fruit’s price.. Image: The glass fruit shatters in a spray of crystalline shards, each fragment catching the ink like tiny mirrors that reflect a disappearing memory..

## Scene 3

Goal: Demonstrate the cost of power with a high‑stakes stroke.. Conflict: To seal a fissure opening in the palace’s marble corridor, Elisa must paint a binding glyph, knowing each stroke steals a memory fragment.. Turn: She paints the glyph, and the ink bites into her skin, leaving a dark, pulsing scar that spells a word she no longer recognizes.. Image: Elisa’s forearm glows with living ink, the scar forming a jagged script that drips and reforms as the marble cracks beneath her..

## Scene 4

Goal: Escalate the chorus’s menace and reveal the half‑remembered lullaby.. Conflict: An echo‑phage swarm erupts in the palace library, devouring spoken histories; the chorus sings a line that matches the lullaby Elisa can no longer recall.. Turn: Elisa forces the echo‑phages to retreat by whispering a forgotten word that slips from the edge of her mind, buying a momentary silence.. Image: Ink‑black birds with glass eyes circle the library’s vaulted ceiling, while a ghostly melody threads through the air, the words shimmering like ink‑written runes..

## Scene 5

Goal: Force the ultimate sacrifice – the loss of her own name.. Conflict: A rift opens in the throne room; sealing it requires a stroke that will consume the memory of Elisa’s name, erasing her legal identity.. Turn: She accepts, paints the final stroke, and feels the name dissolve from her mind, leaving her with a hollow echo where her name once resonated.. Image: The rift swallows the throne’s golden insignia, and Elisa’s reflection in the ink‑filled floor splits into faceless silhouettes, each missing a name‑shaped void..

## Scene 6

Goal: Leave a fresh, unresolved thread.. Conflict: Maeron disappears into the veil, leaving behind a trail of ink‑etched symbols that may map a route to the Veil’s heart.. Turn: Elisa watches the symbols glow faintly, realizing they could be the key to a permanent seal—or a trap.. Image: A winding path of glowing ink symbols spirals up the palace’s highest tower, disappearing into a sky turned black with living ink..

## Cliffhanger

As the ink‑scar on Elisa’s arm pulses, the whispered lullaby coalesces into a single syllable—an echo of the half‑remembered name—just as a new, uncharted ink vortex opens beyond the palace walls, pulling the ink‑etched map toward its center.
