# Work Order: Passionate Book on Love

## Classification
- **Task type**: Book (20 chapters, >=3000 words each)
- **Audience**: Adult readers seeking depth and emotional resonance; literary‑popular hybrid tone.
- **Primary challenge**: Structural cohesion across many thematic facets of love while maintaining passionate voice.
- **Risk level**: Moderate (large scope, multiple thematic arcs).

## Mode
- **Operating mode**: Small Group (4 members) – sufficient for robust editorial workflow without overstaffing.

## Assigned Roles
| Role | Persona | Responsibilities |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Lead | Tolstoy | Define overall structure, outline each chapter, draft first‑pass prose.
| Reviewer | Austen | Ensure tone, audience suitability, character/authenticity consistency.
| Refiner | Woolf | Polish flow, transitions, lyrical richness, maintain passion.
| Fact‑checker | Borges | Verify historical, cultural, philosophical references; flag anachronisms.
| Finisher | Hemingway | Final word‑count control, clarity, final polish before shipment.

*Note*: While the brief requested a team of 4, the Finisher role is considered a *post‑production* checkpoint and does not count toward the core writing team.

## Output Directory
- **Base path**: `local-output/books/love-passionate/`
- Chapters will be stored as `chapter01.md`, `chapter02.md`, … `chapter20.md`.
- The Italian translation will reside in `local-output/translations/love-passionate/` as `capitolo01_it.md`, etc.

## Primary Risks
1. **Scope creep** – 20 distinct love themes may drift; maintain a master thematic map.
2. **Consistency** – Voice must stay unified across chapters written over weeks.
3. **Fact‑checking load** – Diverse cultural references require thorough verification.

## Handoff Sequence
1. **Lead ➜ Reviewer** – After full draft (≥3000 words).
2. **Reviewer ➜ Refiner** – After tone/audience sign‑off.
3. **Refiner ➜ Fact‑checker** – After stylistic polish.
4. **Fact‑checker ➜ Finisher** – After factual clearance.
5. **Finisher ➜ Publication** – Final approval, move chapter to `canon/` and archive previous drafts in `cemetery/`.

## Definition of Done (per chapter)
- Minimum 3000 words, English only.
- No factual errors (fact‑checked).
- Narrative coherence with preceding chapters.
- Passionate yet accessible tone confirmed by Reviewer.
- All assigned roles have signed off.
- File saved as `chapterXX.md` in the output directory.

## First Action
- **Lead (Tolstoy)** will create a detailed outline for Chapter 1 “Beginnings” and store it as `local-output/books/love-passionate/chapter01_outline.md`.
