# Work Order

**Project:** Original literary novel *Inheritance of Shadows* (working title)

## Team Roles
- **Lead:** `tolstoy` – defines structure, writes first drafts, maintains narrative voice.
- **Reviewer:** `austen` – checks coherence, character consistency, prose style.
- **Critic:** `poe` – probes thematic weaknesses, structural drift, melodrama, abstraction, and repetition.
- **Finisher:** `hemingway` – removes padding, enforces consistency, tightens language, signs off for publication.

## Handoff Sequence
1. **Lead → Reviewer** – Deliver chapter‑by‑chapter outline and draft chapters.
2. **Reviewer → Critic** – Provide reviewed drafts with notes on pacing, style, and any unresolved issues.
3. **Critic → Lead** – Return a critique highlighting the most dangerous assumptions, structural fragilities, and any melodramatic excess.
4. **Lead → Finisher** – Incorporate reviewer and critic feedback, produce polished drafts.
5. **Finisher → Lead** – final language tightening, verification against Definition of Done, and shipment readiness sign‑off.

## Critic Policy
- Actively seek **melodrama**, **over‑abstraction**, **repetition**, and **structural drift**.
- Provide concrete suggestions to curb each identified issue.

## Finisher Policy
- Remove any padding or filler.
- Ensure narrative voice, terminology, and formatting are consistent across the manuscript.
- Verify each chapter meets the minimum **3000‑word** requirement.
- Confirm all translation files are present and aligned with the English source.

## Definition of Done
- Files created under `local-output/books/political-inheritance-novel/`:
  - `00_work_order.md`
  - `01_outline.md`
  - `02_voice_guide.md`
  - `03_character_bible.md`
  - `04_revision_strategy.md`
  - `chapter01.md` … `chapter20.md` (each ≥ 3000 words)
  - `italian/chapter01.it.md` … `italian/chapter20.it.md` (faithful translations)
  - `98_translation_notes.md`
  - `99_manifest.md`
- All files pass the **Revision Strategy** passes.
- No recognizable imitation of any real author; prose is original.
- Narrative coherence and continuity maintained throughout.
