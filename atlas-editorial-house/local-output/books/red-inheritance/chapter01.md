# 01 Chapter 01: The Heir's Return

*(Dark, candlelit corridor of the decaying Voss Estate. Dust motes dance in fractured sunlight filtering through broken stained glass. Elena Voss, 34, in a worn velvet cloak, clutches a sealed letter in her gloves. Her reflection in a dusty mirror reveals hollow eyes that have not seen sunlight for six years.)*

**Opening Scene: The Letter**
Elena examines the letter under a flickering lantern. Its wax seal bears the crest of the Voss family - a crimson rose split by a jagged black blade. She had left this estate six years ago, branded a traitor after her father's death. The letter's wax is melted, as if opened hurriedly - perhaps by her uncle Victor.

**Act 1: The Estate's Decay**
The camera pans to the estate's grandeur in decay: collapsed towers, overgrown gardens choked with weeds, portraits of past Voss patriarchs with hollow faces. Elena moves through the library, reading aloud from a crumbling journal of her father's negotiations with foreign powers. Her voice trembles as she discovers entries about a secret pact with the Kovaći Empire.

**Act 2: The Council's Shadow**
She encounters Clara Raines, the Minister of Culture, who insists Elena must return to the council chambers. Their conversation is tense - Clara's polished speech contrasts with Elena's barely concealed anger. Clara subtly implies Elena's return is orchestrated by Victor Voss.

**Act 3: The Brother's Fury**
Elena is intercepted by her brother Luca in the estate grounds. He wears military commander Dante Riva's insignia beneath his civil coat. Their Embrace is brutal - not sexual, but a clash of wills. "You left us to rot," he screams. "Now you want to steal what's ours?

**Act 4: The Ambassador's Trap**
At the estate's throne room, Elena meets Amara Kovać, the foreign diplomat who has funded her return. Amara offers protection from Luca but demands Elena betray Clara in exchange. Elena sees through the manipulation but is forced to make a choice under Luca's physical pressure.

**Climax of Chapter 1: The Trap Springs**
Luca traps Elena in the estate crypt, revealing documents stolen from her father's safe. Clara's name appears alongside foreign names in a treasonous pact. As Luca prepares to execute her, Elena notices a red thread in Luca's hand - a family heirloom. Suddenly, she understands: this isn't just about power, but about claiming the family's secret bloodline symbol.
