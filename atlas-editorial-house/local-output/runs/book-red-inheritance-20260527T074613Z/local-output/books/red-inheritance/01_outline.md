# Red Inheritance - Outline

## Theme
Power, betrayal

## Genre
Literary novel

## Chapter Structure
20 chapters, each with minimum 3000 words

## Chapter Summaries

1. The Catalyst - The initial incident that sets the story in motion. Introduction of central family and stakes.
2. Seeds of Doubt - Early tensions and hints of betrayal.
3. Lines Drawn - Clear alliances and enmities form.
4. The Power Play - First major power struggle escalates.
5. The Outsider - Introduction of an unpredictable element.
6. Cracks Appear - Fault lines in relationships widen.
7. Secrets Unveiled - Revelations change the dynamics.
8. The Fallout - Consequences of betrayals begin to unfold.
9. Turning Point - Major event shifts power balance.
10. Shadows of the Past - Backstory and motivations revealed.
11. The Reckoning - Characters confront their choices.
12. Fractures - Further divisions and fractures among factions.
13. The Betrayer - The true nature of betrayal exposed.
14. Retribution - Acts of revenge and counterplays.
15. Collapse - The family, alliances, and power structures unravel.
16. New Orders - Attempts to establish new control.
17. The Price - Paying the cost of betrayal and power.
18. Redemption - Characters seek forgiveness or justice.
19. The Last Stand - Final confrontations and resolutions.
20. Legacy - Aftermath and the lasting impact of the events.

---

This outline sets the narrative arc emphasizing tension, betrayal, and power dynamics to ensure a coherent novel structure.
