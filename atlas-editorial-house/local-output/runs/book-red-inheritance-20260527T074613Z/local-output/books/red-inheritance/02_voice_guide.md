# Red Inheritance - Voice and Style Guide

## Narrative Voice
- Third-person limited omniscient perspective
- Focus on internal states and complex emotional landscapes
- Use of subtle irony and restrained eloquence

## Tone
- Serious and somber with moments of intense emotional conflict
- Elegant prose that balances vivid imagery with clarity
- Underlying tension and unease present in dialogue and description

## Style
- Sentence structure varies between flowing descriptive passages and terse dialogue
- Precise vocabulary to evoke power dynamics and betrayal
- Symbolism and motifs weave consistently to reinforce themes

## Character Voice
- Distinctive speech patterns based on background and personality
- Dialogue to reveal underlying motives and conflicts subtly

## Consistency Principles
- Maintain thematic coherence in language use
- Avoid clichés and melodrama
- Use realistic, psychologically driven character reactions

---

This guide ensures the literary quality and cohesion of the narrative voice across all chapters.
