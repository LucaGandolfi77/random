# Red Inheritance - Character Bible

## Main Characters

### Elena Vashkevich
- Role: Matriarch of the Vashkevich family
- Traits: Ambitious, calculating, fiercely protective
- Arc: Struggles with maintaining family control amid emerging betrayals

### Dmitri Vashkevich
- Role: Elena's eldest son, heir apparent
- Traits: Charismatic but ruthless, politically savvy
- Arc: Power-hungry, conflicted about loyalty and legacy

### Anna Petrova
- Role: Dmitri's wife, outsider to the family
- Traits: Intelligent, discreet, morally complex
- Arc: Navigates delicate balance between alliance and self-preservation

### Sergei Vashkevich
- Role: Younger brother, rebellious and idealistic
- Traits: Impulsive, passionate, driven by justice
- Arc: Opposes Dmitri's methods, becomes a wildcard in family struggle

### Viktor Baranov
- Role: Family advisor and longtime confidant
- Traits: Loyal but pragmatic, harbors secrets
- Arc: Balances personal loyalty with survival instincts

## Supporting Characters

- Various political, business, and social figures influencing family dynamics

## Relationships & Conflicts

- Elena-Dmitri: Power struggle for family control
- Dmitri-Anna: Marriage strained by secrets and ambitions
- Dmitri-Sergei: Sibling rivalry escalating to betrayal
- Elena-Viktor: Unspoken mutual dependence and mistrust

## Symbolism
- The "Red Inheritance": Metaphor for the dangerous legacy of power and betrayal
- Family estate: Physical and symbolic center of power
