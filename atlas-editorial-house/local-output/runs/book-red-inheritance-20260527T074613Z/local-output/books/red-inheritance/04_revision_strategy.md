# Red Inheritance - Revision Strategy

## Revision Phases

1. **Initial Draft Review**
   - Check minimum word count (3000 words per chapter)
   - Basic plot and factual consistency check

2. **Critical Assessment**
   - Named risks identified per chapter
   - Evaluate character motivations, pacing, and thematic coherence

3. **Stylistic Refinement**
   - Enforce narrative voice and style guide
   - Remove padding and redundancies
   - Normalize terminology and symbolism

4. **Coherence and Continuity Check**
   - Ensure consistency across chapters (character, timeline, motifs)
   - Update character bible as needed

5. **Final Sign-Off**
   - Critic and finisher approval
   - Deliver polished chapter for inclusion

## Review Team Roles

- **Critic**: Identifies risks and weaknesses, suggests improvements
- **Finisher**: Ensures polish, style consistency, and terminological normalization

## Tools and Documentation

- All phases documented with specific feedback and corrective measures
- Use version control for drafts and revisions

---

This structured revision approach aims to ensure thematic integrity, clarity, and literary quality throughout the novel development process.
