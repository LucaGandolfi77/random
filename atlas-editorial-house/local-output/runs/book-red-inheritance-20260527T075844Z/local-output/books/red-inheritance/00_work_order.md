# Work Order for "Red Inheritance"

## Project Overview
- Title: Red Inheritance
- Theme: Power, betrayal
- Genre: Literary novel
- Length: 20 chapters, minimum 3,000 words each
- Languages: English and Italian (translation after English manuscript completion)

## Team Roles
- Director: Oversees the entire project workflow, ensures consistency and quality.
- Planner: Develops the outline, voice guide, character bible, and revision strategy.
- Writer: Drafts each chapter in English according to the outline and voice guide.
- Critic: Reviews each chapter for narrative risks, thematic coherence, and plausibility.
- Finisher: Polishes language, removes padding, and normalizes terminology.
- Translator: Translates completed chapters into Italian, adhering to translation notes.
- Editorial Assistant: Updates character bible, tracks progress, and manages files.

## Work Route
1. Assemble the team and write this Work Order.
2. Produce planning documents:
   - 01_outline.md
   - 02_voice_guide.md
   - 03_character_bible.md
   - 04_revision_strategy.md
3. Draft chapter 1 following planning guidance.
4. Review and critique chapter 1.
5. Refine and finish chapter 1, then write to disk.
6. Translate chapter 1 into Italian.
7. Repeat steps 3-6 for each subsequent chapter (2–20).
8. After all chapters are complete and translated, finalize translation notes.
9. Write the final manifest (99_manifest.md) recording all deliverables.

## Definition of Done
- All 20 chapters exist as complete files with at least 3,000 words each in English.
- Each chapter has a corresponding Italian translation.
- The critic identifies at least one specific risk per chapter upon sign-off.
- The finisher's version is polished and consistent in terminology.
- Planning documents are comprehensive and guide the novel coherently.
- The novel reads as a unified literary work, not disconnected episodes.
- Character, timeline, and thematic continuity are maintained throughout.
- Translation notes capture key adaptation decisions.
- The manifest accurately inventories all deliverables.
