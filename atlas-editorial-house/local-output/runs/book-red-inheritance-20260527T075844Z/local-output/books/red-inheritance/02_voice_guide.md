# Voice Guide for "Red Inheritance"

## Narrative Style
- Literary fiction with an emphasis on psychological depth and nuanced character portrayal.
- Prose to be elegant but accessible, balancing descriptive richness with clear, direct language.
- Use varied sentence structures: mix of complex, reflective passages and short, impactful sentences.

## Tone
- Serious and somber, reflecting themes of power and betrayal.
- Moments of tension and suspense balanced with introspective or emotional depth.
- Avoid melodrama; favor subtlety and restraint.

## Point of View
- Primarily third-person limited, focalized through major characters to provide insight into their internal conflicts.
- Occasional shifts to other perspectives to reveal contradictions and deepen complexity.

## Dialogue
- Naturalistic but purposeful, revealing character and advancing plot.
- Use regional or sociolect differences sparingly to differentiate characters.

## Symbolism
- Recurring motifs related to legacy, blood, and power disparities.
- Use symbolism to subtly reinforce themes without heavy-handedness.

## Style Consistency
- Maintain uniform voice across chapters despite multiple authors or drafts.
- Careful editing to ensure voice cohesion throughout the work.
