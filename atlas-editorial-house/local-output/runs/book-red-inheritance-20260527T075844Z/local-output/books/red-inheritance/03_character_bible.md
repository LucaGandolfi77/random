# Character Bible for "Red Inheritance"

## Main Characters

### Elijah Redmond
- Role: Protagonist
- Description: Heir to the Redmond family legacy; ambitious, idealistic but increasingly disillusioned.
- Age: Early 30s
- Traits: Intelligent, introspective, conflicted about family duties.
- Arc: Transforms from hopeful successor to a figure grappling with the moral cost of power.

### Vivienne Redmond
- Role: Elijah's sister, rival
- Description: Charismatic, cunning, and willing to betray family ties for personal gain.
- Age: Late 20s
- Traits: Ambitious, manipulative, strategically minded.
- Arc: Seeks to unsettle Elijah's inheritance while masking vulnerabilities.

### Marcus Fenway
- Role: Antagonist, external rival
- Description: Business magnate seeking to undermine the Redmond family’s power.
- Age: Mid-40s
- Traits: Ruthless, resourceful, cold.
- Arc: Represents outside pressure and threat to family cohesion.

## Supporting Characters

### Eleanor Redmond
- Role: Matriarch
- Description: Stern, authoritative, the keeper of family secrets.
- Age: 60s
- Traits: Wise, secretive, traditional.
- Role in plot: Catalyst for revelations affecting family dynamics.

### Noah Grant
- Role: Confidant to Elijah
- Description: Loyal friend and advisor, pragmatic and trustworthy.
- Age: Early 30s
- Traits: Grounded, supportive.

### Various family members and allies
- Purpose: Provide depth, illustrate loyalty and betrayal nuances.

## Institutions
- Redmond Family Estate and Business
- Fenway Corporation
