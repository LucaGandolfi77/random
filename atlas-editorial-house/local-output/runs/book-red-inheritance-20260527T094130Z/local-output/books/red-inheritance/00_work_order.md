# Red Inheritance Work Order

## Project Team
- Director: Oversees all stages from planning to publication.
- Planner: Develops outline, voice guide, character bible, and revision strategy.
- Writer: Drafts chapters based on the outline and voice guide.
- Critic: Reviews each chapter for risks, thematic accuracy, and coherence.
- Finisher: Polishes language, normalizes terminology, removes padding.
- Translator: Translates completed chapters into Italian.
- Translation Reviewer: Checks accuracy and tone of translations.

## Project Route
1. Complete planning documents: outline, voice guide, character bible, revision strategy.
2. Draft chapters 01 through 20 in order, each to a minimum of 3000 words.
3. Critique and refine each chapter before moving to the next.
4. Translate each completed English chapter into Italian, noting translation decisions.
5. Revise and finalize translations.
6. Final revision pass maintaining continuity and thematic coherence.
7. Compile manifest and documentation.

## Definition of Done
- All 20 English chapters completed, reviewed, refined, and finalized.
- All chapters translated into Italian with corresponding notes.
- Planning documents completed and stored.
- Revision strategy followed with risks identified and language normalized.
- Files organized under local-output/books/red-inheritance/ as specified.

---

All phases proceed sequentially. No phase ends without full adherence to quality and completeness criteria.

Director has final approval authority on quality and completion.
