# Red Inheritance - Voice Guide

## Voice and Tone
- Serious, elegant, and emotionally resonant.
- Nuanced and reflective narrative style.
- Avoid caricature and vague praise.
- Emphasize clarity, coherence, and practical usefulness.
- Literary novel conventions: rich characterization, deep internal conflict, symbolic motifs.

## Stylistic Guidelines
- Use precise, evocative language without ornamental excess.
- Maintain a balanced narrative pace, blending exposition and dialogue.
- Present perspectives empathetically, showing multiple facets of character motivations.
- Keep thematic elements subtly woven through plot and dialogue.
- Avoid clichés and predictable tropes.

## Emotional Depth
- Explore psychological complexity of characters.
- Show moral ambiguity and conflicting loyalties.
- Use symbolism and metaphor to enhance thematic impact.

---

This guide ensures consistent voice throughout the book, supporting the theme of political power, family betrayal, and institutional decline.
