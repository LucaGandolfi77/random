# Red Inheritance - Character Bible

## Main Characters

### Victor Redford
- Patriarch of the Redford family.
- Established the family's political dynasty.
- Authoritarian yet charismatic.
- Struggles to maintain control over family and institutions amid growing decay.

### Elena Redford
- Ambitious eldest daughter and heir apparent.
- Brilliant strategist, poised to succeed Victor.
- Torn between loyalty to family and her own political vision.

### Marcus Redford
- Middle son, career military officer.
- Loyal to family but conflicted by military duty to the state.
- Embodies discipline and honor, but harbors doubts about family ethics.

### Julian Redford
- Youngest son, rebellious and idealistic.
- Critical of the family's political legacy.
- Seeks change but struggles with his place within the family.

### Claudia Varnum
- Senior advisor and confidante to Victor.
- Skilled political operator with ambiguous loyalties.

### Elias Marlow
- Opposing senator and political rival.
- Catalyst for much of the familys external challenges.

## Family and Political Dynamics
- The Redford family wields considerable political power but is riven by internal tensions.
- Betrayals and alliances shift across chapters, enhancing conflict.
- Institutional decline is mirrored in fracturing familial relationships.
