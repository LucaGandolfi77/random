# Red Inheritance - Work Order

## Team Composition

- Director Agent: Oversees project workflow, maintains continuity, and enforces quality standards.
- Literary Editor: Focused on narrative quality, theme consistency, character development.
- Technical Editor: Ensures language precision, formatting, and editorial correctness.
- Translator: Expert in Italian translation with political and emotional text sensitivity.
- Critic: Provides feedback on risks and continuity issues.
- Finisher: Removes padding, normalizes terminology, and ensures narrative flow.

## Route

1. Assemble team and define work order.
2. Produce planning docs (outline, voice guide, character bible, revision strategy).
3. Draft each chapter sequentially (minimum 3000 words each).
4. Review, critique, and refine each chapter before moving on.
5. Translate each chapter into Italian after finalizing the English version.
6. Document translation notes.
7. Complete final manifest capturing all deliverables.

## Definition of Done

- All planning documents created and approved.
- Each chapter is at least 3000 words, coherent with overall narrative.
- Critic identifies at least one risk per chapter and signs off.
- Finisher normalizes terminology and removes unnecessary padding.
- Italian translations are faithful, respecting tone and context.
- Translation notes clarify key translation decisions.
- Manifest completed with all files recorded.
