# Red Inheritance - Voice Guide

## Narrative Voice
- Third-person limited omniscient
- Focus on internal thoughts and perspectives of key characters
- Balanced between lyrical prose and clear, precise language

## Tone
- Serious, introspective, and contemplative
- Underlying tension reflecting political and familial conflict
- Occasional moments of stark realism and emotional intensity

## Style
- Medium to long sentences for reflective passages
- Short, sharp sentences for tension and conflict scenes
- Symbolism used subtly to reinforce themes
- Avoid ornate or florid language; prefer clarity and impact

## Emotional Register
- Complex emotions: betrayal, ambition, regret, despair
- Avoid melodrama; focus on authentic human reactions

## Dialogue
- Realistic and purposeful
- Reflects social and political context
- Differentiated by character background and status

## Consistency
- Maintain continuity in voice across chapters
- Smooth transitions between scenes and perspectives

---

This voice guide will help ensure a coherent and engaging reading experience throughout the novel.
