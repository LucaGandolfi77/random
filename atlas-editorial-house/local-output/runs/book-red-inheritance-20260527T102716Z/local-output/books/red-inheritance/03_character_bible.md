# Red Inheritance - Character Bible

## Main Characters

### 1. Victor Caruso
- Patriarch of the Caruso family
- Head of a powerful political dynasty
- Calculating, authoritative, with a hidden vulnerability
- Represents the old guard of political power

### 2. Elena Caruso
- Victor's wife
- Resilient, politically savvy
- Balances family loyalty with personal ambition

### 3. Marco Caruso
- Eldest son
- Ambitious, ruthless
- Seeks to inherit control but struggles with moral boundaries

### 4. Bianca Caruso
- Marco's sister
- Intelligent and idealistic
- Represents the hope for reform but conflicted by family ties

### 5. Alessandro Rossi
- Political rival
- Charismatic, strategic
- Catalyst for family betrayal and institutional decline

### 6. Lucia Ferri
- Family confidante and advisor
- Loyal but conflicted about the family's ethics

### Supporting Characters
- Various political figures, family members, and institutional actors

## Relationships
- Complex dynamics of loyalty, betrayal, and power between family members
- External pressures from rivals and declining institutions

## Institutions
- The Caruso political dynasty
- Local government and political bodies
- Family businesses intertwined with politics
