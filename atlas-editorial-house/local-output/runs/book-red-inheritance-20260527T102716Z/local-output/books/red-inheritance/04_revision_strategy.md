# Red Inheritance - Revision Strategy

## Revision Phases

1. **Initial Draft Completion**
   - Ensure chapter meets minimum word count (3000 words).
   - Check for narrative coherence and alignment with outline.

2. **Critical Review**
   - Critic identifies at least one specific risk or weakness.
   - Feedback focuses on plot, character consistency, pacing, and thematic clarity.

3. **Editing and Refinement**
   - Technical editor addresses language clarity, continuity, and style consistency.
   - Incorporate critic feedback to improve narrative and structure.

4. **Finalization**
   - Finisher normalizes terminology, removes padding and redundancies.
   - Ensure smooth flow and coherent reading experience.

5. **Approval**
   - Chapter finalized only when all roles sign off.

## Process
- No next chapter begins until current chapter is fully approved.
- Updates to the character bible and other planning docs occur as needed.
- Italian translation begins post English final approval.

## Quality Checks
- Word count verification.
- Thematic and narrative continuity.
- Risk assessment and mitigation.
- Terminology standardization.

---

This strategy ensures each chapter is polished and aligned with the overall vision before advancing.
