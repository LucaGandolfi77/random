# Chronicle of Morrowfield

## Morrowfield, 1901: Contracts and Lanterns

Mercy Bell died in autumn when work, worry, and weather at last agreed against the body, and Bell Night lit the cemetery so that widows and liars sang together as witnesses. Caleb Vale and Miriam Harrow turned careful courtship into a public marriage, and Arthur Harrow arrived so that every old loyalty in Morrowfield bent around the cradle, even as Silas Vale sharpened an old grievance against Jonah Harrow into a new public scene. The church staged Harvest Supper where compliments sounded like contracts, and the Lantern Regatta let the river carry lanterns as if grudges might drift away; the Thaw Market reopened credit and appetite, and old arguments returned with the river road.

## Morrowfield, 1904: Weather, Work, and Words

In 1904, Morrowfield let work, worry, and weather carry Gideon Bell in spring and Frank Harrow in autumn, while the same Frank’s birth once bent every old loyalty around a cradle. Silas Vale let a rumor about Jonah Harrow’s affairs stride into the market, and Jonah twice sharpened an old grievance against Silas into public noise; meanwhile, a ribbon-bound packet of letters behind the Blue Lantern had half the town rewriting memory as credit and appetite returned at the Thaw Market, Bell Night lit the cemetery for widows and liars, and the Lantern Regatta set the river drifting with forgiving light.
