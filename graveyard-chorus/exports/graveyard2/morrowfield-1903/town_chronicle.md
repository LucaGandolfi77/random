# Chronicle of Morrowfield

## Morrowfield 1901: Contracts and Lanterns

Mercy Bell was carried into town memory as autumn pressed work, worry, and weather against the body, while Caleb Vale and Miriam Harrow turned careful courtship into a civic marriage under public scrutiny. Arthur Harrow arrived carrying several families at once, and every old loyalty in Morrowfield bent around the cradle, even as Silas Vale renewed a family bitterness against Jonah Harrow. The church staged Harvest Supper where compliments sounded like contracts, the town hall reopened credit and appetite at the Thaw Market, Bell Night made widows and liars sing together at the cemetery, and the Lantern Regatta let the river look forgiving for a night.

- Mood: caustic
- Public actors: Ada Quill, Arthur Harrow, Caleb Vale, Eliza Harrow, Jonah Harrow, Mercy Bell, Miriam Harrow, Ruth Vale

## : Autumn's Sharp Echoes in Morrowfield

Ruth Vale died in autumn as work, worry, and weather finally overcame her. Throughout the year, Ruth and Jonah Harrow traded sharp rumors and renewed old grievances, while Silas Vale spread a tale of Jonah's affairs that reached the market by noon. The town council revisited the sluice dispute at town hall, a heat spell overwhelmed the clinic with the smell of vinegar and impatience, and the Harvest Supper at the church turned compliments into contracts and silence into weight.

- Mood: caustic
- Public actors: Ada Quill, Caleb Vale, Eliza Harrow, Jonah Harrow, Miriam Harrow, Ruth Vale, Silas Vale, Thomas Quill
