### Cradles, Rumors, and Old Grievances in 1908 Morrowfield

Morrowfield, 1908: Bennett Harrow arrives
and the ledger of loyalty tilts.
Caleb Vale and Miriam Harrow listen,
each to an inward verdict, iron in the throat.
Silas Vale strikes the flint of bitterness
until the Harrow name spits sparks;
Jonah Harrow lets rumor lace its feet
and by noon the market wears the lie.
Behind the Blue Lantern, a box of letters
asks the town to revise its memory.
The council reopens the sluice dispute
as if water remembers who owned it.
A heat spell drives the clinic past mercy;
Thomas Quill keeps his daily labor.
Eliza Harrow carries Miriam's burden
while widows and liars gather at Bell Night,
voice against stone, singing the graves awake.

### Thomas Quill
